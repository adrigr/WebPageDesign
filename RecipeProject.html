Coffee Cake

This is a quick little delicious coffee cake for any day!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 3 tsp. baking powder
  • 4 tbs. butter

Directions

  1. Mix sugar, cinnamon, salt, egg, and milk and beat thoroughly
  2. Add flour, baking powder, and butter
  3. Turn into lightly greased nine-inch cake pan
  4. Sprinkle with three tablespoons sugar and one tsp. cinnamon which has been mixed together
  5. Add nuts to topping if desired
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes

Serve warm with a nice glass of milk and enjoy!
